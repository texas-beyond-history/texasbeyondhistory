Texas Beyond History is a resource dedicated to helping educators and students learn more about the unique, multi-layered history of Texas. Using archeology as a lens for discovery and learning, this website offers engaging TEKS-based lesson plans for both elementary and secondary grade levels.

All lesson plans found on the TBH website were crafted by experienced teachers or consultants, and may be downloaded and printed for easy classroom use. There is also a Guide to help you find additional resources on TBH covering a variety of subjects. Welcome to the fun and excitement of teaching about Texas' cultural heritage, archeology, and many other fascinating topics!

In addition, don't miss the Kids Only, where you and your students will find a wealth of educational games and engaging activities, many led by Dr. Dirt, The Armadillo Archeologist. Finally, the Research section will guide your students through the process of using TBH as a resource to research and write a report.
