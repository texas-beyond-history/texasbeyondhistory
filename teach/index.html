Welcome Teachers!

Texas Beyond History is a resource dedicated to helping educators and students learn about the history of Texas. Using archeology as a lens for discovery and learning, we publish engaging TEKS-based lesson plans for both elementary and secondary grade levels.

Click on your subject area to find archeology-related lesson plans.

All lesson plans found on the TBH website were written by experienced teachers or consultants, and can be downloaded and printed for easy classroom use. Check out the Teachers' Guide to help you find additional resources on TBH covering a variety of subjects, including Kids' interactive web activities. Welcome to the fun and excitement of teaching about Texas' cultural heritage, archeology, and many other fascinating topics!

Don't miss TBH Kids, where you and your students will find a wealth of educational games and engaging activities, many led by Dr. Dirt, The Armadillo Archeologist. Visit the Research section will guide your students through the process of using TBH as a resource to research and write a report.
