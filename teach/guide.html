Teacher's Guide to Texas Beyond History

Teaching about Texas Indians? Early Explorers? European settlers? African American freedmen? Here's a handy list of lessons and interactive student activities on Texas Beyond History that correspond to these topics. And for geography, look below for links to virtual field trips and galleries of natural resources on TBH. The guide provides guidance for level (elementary and secondary) but many lessons can be adapted to work for both.

E = Elementary grade level, S = Secondary grade level
