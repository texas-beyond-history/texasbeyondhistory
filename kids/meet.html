Meet You at the Midden Board Game

 

A Map Game by Marilyn Eisenwine

Click on the picture to the right to open up the game materials in Adobe PDF format (if this doesn't work, or if you don't have Adobe Reader, ask your parents if you can download Adobe Reader for free). Print all four pages and cut out each of the game cards. Find a game piece to move along the trail (a small rock or penny works great). Use dice or a spinner to move your game piece along the trail. If you land on a dart point along the trail you must draw a card and follow the instructions. If the card says to go to a place such as the river or the hut, you must go to the space on the trail that is nearest to that location. The winner must roll the exact number to get on the final dart point at the Finish. Good luck!
