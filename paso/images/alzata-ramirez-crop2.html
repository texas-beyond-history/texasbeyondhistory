After the Pueblo Revolt of 1680, a large number of Tigua, Piro, Tompiro, Tano and Jemez refugees and hostages accompanied the Spanish to El Paso. Governor Otermín founded the missions of Corpus Christi de la Ysleta, San Antonio de Senecú and Nuestra Señora de la Limpia Conceptión del Socorro for them in 1682.
